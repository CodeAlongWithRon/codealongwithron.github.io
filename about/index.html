Who am I?

Let me introduce myself.

Hi! My name is Ron. I’m a 34 year old .net developer from the Netherlands. Throughout my career I have mainly done consultancy jobs, but currently I’m working as a full stack .net developer at a university.

I first started coding before the age of ten and have been intrigued by it ever since. The skill to turn an idea into a working piece of software is something that I can get extremely enthusiastic about. In this blog I want to take you along with me as I work on various projects. My main goal is to share my knowledge with you, but also to learn from the feedback you provide. Coding is an endless learning journey and I would love to have you along the ride with me.
